# Haven — Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 4, 2026

## Summary
Haven does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Period.

## What Haven Does NOT Do
- Haven does NOT upload your files or file metadata to any server
- Haven does NOT collect usage analytics or telemetry
- Haven does NOT require an account or login
- Haven does NOT access the internet for any purpose other than Microsoft Store licensing verification
- Haven does NOT share any data with third parties

## What Haven Does
- Haven scans directories on your local machine to classify and organize files
- Haven stores scan results, settings, and staging data locally on your PC in %APPDATA%/Haven/
- Haven uses Microsoft Store APIs to verify your license status (free vs Pro)

## Data Storage
All Haven data is stored locally:
- Settings: SharedPreferences (local storage)
- Staged files: %USERPROFILE%/.haven_staging/
- Debug log (if enabled): %APPDATA%/Haven/haven_debug.log

## Your Rights
You can delete all Haven data at any time by:
1. Uninstalling Haven from Windows Settings
2. Deleting %APPDATA%/Haven/ and %USERPROFILE%/.haven_staging/
